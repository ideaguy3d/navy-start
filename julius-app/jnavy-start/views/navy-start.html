Define the mission of the Navy.

The mission of the United States Navy is to protect and defend the right of the United States and our allies to move freely on the oceans and protect our country against her enemies.

Navy Core Values

Define Honor.

"I will bear true faith and allegiance..." I am accountable for my professional and personal behavior. I will be mindful of the privilege I have to serve my fellow Americans.
